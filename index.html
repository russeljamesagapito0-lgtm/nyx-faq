How to refer a client

You get a leads database, do the outreach, and earn — whether you close it yourself or hand it off to me.

Step 01
Get your leads
You'll receive a database of potential business leads to work from. No need to hunt for them yourself.
Step 02
Reach out
Contact the business directly via FB or IG DM. Introduce yourself and spark interest in getting a website.
Step 03
Close or hand off
Close the deal yourself or pass it to me for technical questions. Either way — same 20% commission for you.
Step 04
Get paid
Once the client sends their 50% downpayment, your commission is released — via GCash or PayPal.
Earnings breakdown

Your commission

Simple, transparent, and fair. No hidden rules — just 20% of every package you bring in.

💰
You earn 20% of the package price for every client you refer that proceeds.
⏱
Commission is released after the client complete their payment — within 24 hours of completion.
🤝
Same 20% whether you close the deal yourself or hand it off to me.
🛡
If the client backs out after paying, the downpayment is non-refundable — and you still get your commission.
✕
Commission does not apply to retainers or add-ons — only the main package price.
